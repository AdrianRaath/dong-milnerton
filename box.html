Meet the Box

The DongFeng Box redefines urban mobility with its bold, distinctive design and compact footprint. Perfect for city driving, this all-electric vehicle combines playful aesthetics with practical performance.

With a range that handles your daily commute and weekend adventures, the Box delivers everything you need in an eco-friendly package that stands out from the crowd.
